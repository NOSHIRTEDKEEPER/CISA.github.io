Who We Are

Welcome to the Cybersecurity and Infrastructure Security Agency (CISA), a vital part of the Department of Homeland Security (DHS). Our mission is to lead the national effort to protect and strengthen America’s cyber and physical infrastructure. From securing elections to defending power grids, CISA ensures the systems you rely on every day are safe and resilient.

Our Goals:

  • Protect federal networks from cyber threats.
  • Strengthen critical infrastructure like energy and transportation.
  • Coordinate emergency communications during crises.
  • Partner with government, businesses, and citizens to build a secure future.

CISA’s work touches every aspect of modern life. Here’s how we protect you:

Cybersecurity

Infrastructure Security

Emergency Communications

We ensure first responders stay connected during disasters, keeping communities safe.

Our Impact: Strengthening National Security

CISA is a cornerstone of homeland security, protecting the systems Americans rely on daily. We prevent cyber attacks, enhance public safety, and build resilience across critical infrastructure. Without CISA, our digital and physical infrastructure would be vulnerable to adversaries like nation-state hackers and cybercriminals.

Did you know? CISA secures elections, stops ransomware, and ensures power stays on during crises.
